Channel letters

Home / Signs Source USA / Channel letters

Channel letters are one of the most common types of signage. As the name suggests, channel letters, or dimensional letters, are individually fabricated letters, which are frequently internally lit. Sign Source USA is a leading manufacturer of channel letters for companies all across the country. There are many different styles to choose from, giving you tremendous flexibility. This handy poster shows you the different types of letters.

Economy LED Channel Letters

Our economy LED channel letters are wildly popular. They’re only $4.95/inch. Ask us for details and check out our flyer.

Standard Pre-Finished Cans and Faces

Our standard pre-finished cans and faces come with .050 alum back, .040 alum returns, 3/16″ plex faces, 1″ jewelite, ¼–20 rivet nuts, and 1/2″ nipple out the back and mounting templates. Any color, size or font.

Standard Pre-Finished Channel Letters

Our standard pre-finished channel letter comes with .050 alum back, .040 alum returns, 3/16″ plex faces, 1″ jewelite, ¼–20 rivet nuts, and 1/2″ nipple out the back, mounting template and customer’s lighting wired in by Sign Source USA, Inc. Standard Pre-finished Channel Letters can come in any color, size or font.

Facelit Remote Channel Letters

Facelit remote channel letters can be manufactured in almost any font, color or size. This makes channel letters an extremely flexible and attractive product. Our standard pre-finished channel letters consists of .050 alum back, .040 alum returns, 3/16″ plex faces, 1″ jewelite, ¼–20 rivet nuts, ul labeled and 1/2″ nipple out the back. They come complete with transformers & mounting templates.

Reverse Fabricated Non-Illuminated Channel Letters

Non-illuminated reverse fabricated letters are custom fabricated with aluminum faces and welded returns. These letters are complete and ready to install with straps in the back for mounting or we can fit it with a lexan back if the application is needed.

Self-Contained Channel Letters

Self-contained channel letters are delivered complete and ready to install with either neon or a led transformer inside. They include a toggle switch on the side and power whip out the back. They are custom fabricated to the depth you need. Our standard depth is 8″. They come with 3/16″ plex face, jewelite trim cap, or aluminum retainer and aluminum backs and returns.

LED Channel Letters

LED channel letters are standard pre-finished channel letters with your specified brand or Sign Source USA, Inc. brand of led installed inside the letters. This letter comes complete with ul label, .050 back, 040 return, 3/16″ plex face, 1″ jewelite, ¼–20 rivet nuts, 1/2″ nipple, trans, and mounting template. Signs are complete and ready to install. Ask about our economy LED channel letters – only $4.95/inch!

Facelit Raceway Channel Letters

Raceway mounted channel letters are the same letter that you receive in the remote option; however it is attached to a custom fabricated raceway to hold the letters. Raceways come with a lip on top for mounting through and lifting bars inside the raceway.

Open Face Channel Letters

Open face channel letters are custom fabricated channel letters without faces. We can fabricate any depth you require. Our standard is 3″ deep with painted insides and out with exposed neon inside to punctuate your letters. These can be remote, on a raceway, self-contained, or mounted to a cabinet.

Backlit Channel Letters

Sign Source USA, Inc.’s backlit channel letters are custom fabricated aluminum letters—welded, cleaned, and painted—ready to install. We fit our backlit channel letters, aluminum faces, & aluminum returns with clear lexan backs including either neon or led mounted inside. Our backlit letters come with a sealtite connector and rivet nuts for easy installation.
