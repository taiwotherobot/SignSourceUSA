Cabinet signs

Home / Signs Source USA / Cabinet signs

Single Face

Sign Source USA, Inc. can custom fabricate your single face sign to fit any shape you need: square, rectangle, oval or custom contoured shape. We can either hand fabricate or use standard extrusion to create your sign. Signs are complete with lighting and face. We can provide you with many face options: flat lexan, aluminum routed out, flex face, pan formed face, or no face. We can set up for wall mount, pole mount, or hanging.

Double Face

Our double face sign cabinets can be built to any size. We can custom fabricate to any depth or use standard extrusion to create your sign. Signs are complete with lighting and face. We can provide fluorescent, HID, or LED lighting. We can set up double faces to mount however you specify: match plate, single pole, double pole, between poles, hanging, or other method.
